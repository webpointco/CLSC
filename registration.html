Eligibility

Any student between the ages of 4 and 18 at the beginning of the school year is eligible to enroll at CLSC. Exceptions may be made on an individual basis by the Principal, who is responsible for reviewing the enrollment application and placement of the students.

New Student Trial Session

Prospective students can try out our classes at no cost. Please reach out to our Academic Dean at clsc.dean@clscweb.org for further information.

Late Registration / Entering School

Students who enroll in CLSC four weeks after the semester’s start date will have their tuition pro-rated but will still pay for registration and material fees.

How to Register

New Students

Families of new students need to download and complete the CLSC new student registration form shown below. Once completed, please e-mail the form to our Principal at clsc.principal@clscweb.org. Please reach out to the Principal should you have any questions. We will contact you after receipt and review of your form.

New Student Register Here

Please contact the Principal at clsc.principal@clscweb.org with any questions or concerns. We will contact you after your form is reviewed and approved.

Current Students

All families of returning students should register for a new semester using the Registration Form shown below. This form can also be used to update your family information. If you need further assistance, please contact the CLSC Registrar via email at clsc.registrar@clscweb.org. Please include your Family ID in the subject line.

Payment

CLSC accepts the following forms of payment.

Zelle Transfer

Instruction for payment by Zelle:

1) Send your payment to payment@clscweb.org.

2) Include your Family ID, the student(s) name, and your phone number in the Comments section of the transfer form.

Click here for instructions to set-up a Zelle payment.

* Note: CLSC encourages everyone to pay via Zelle. Accordingly, we offer a one-time rebate of $5 per family when they first pay by Zelle, which will be credited as a discount towards the following semester’s tuition.
